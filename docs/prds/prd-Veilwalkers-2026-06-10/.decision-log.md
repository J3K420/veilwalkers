## 2026-06-10 — Run start

- **Intent:** Create. No prior PRD existed.
- **Inputs ingested (extracted):** `docs/game-design-doc.md` (v1.0), `CLAUDE.md` (project conventions + hard requirements + credit economy).
- **Working mode chosen:** Fast path. Rationale: a detailed, internally-consistent game design doc already exists; the productive move is to draft the full PRD from it and tag inferences as `[ASSUMPTION]` rather than re-elicit from scratch.
- **Stakes calibration:** Launch (public Google Play release, real-money IAP). Full rigor on Constraints & Guardrails (AR safety, privacy, billing) and Cross-Cutting NFRs.
- **Form-factor:** Mobile, Android native (Unity + AR Foundation/ARCore). Single-surface.
- **Scope decision:** PRD authored to MVP (Phase 1) depth; Phase 2/3 captured as Out-of-Scope-for-MVP and Roadmap notes, not as FRs.

### Template shape decisions
- **Spine:** full Essential Spine retained.
- **Adapt-In clusters pulled in** (driven by product concerns):
  - Aesthetic & Tone — branded consumer product with a strong visual identity.
  - Information Architecture — multiple top-level surfaces (Home, AR Hunt, Codex, Pit, Shop).
  - Monetization — real-money credit packs via Play Billing; core to the business.
  - Platform — native Android / ARCore device support is a hard gate.
  - Cross-Cutting NFRs — performance on mid-range devices is make-or-break for AR immersion.
  - Constraints & Guardrails (Safety / Privacy / Cost) — AR safety warning and camera disclosure are Google Play hard requirements.
- **Clusters dropped:** Enterprise, Regulated-domain, Developer-product, Embedded — not applicable to a consumer game.

### Key authoring decisions
- **The Pit** classified as a *signature differentiator but post-MVP* (design doc presents it as added depth; CLAUDE.md core loop is Lure→Scan/Capture/Slay→Codex→Shop). Captured as a full feature spec under §4 but marked `[NON-GOAL for MVP]` so it survives to v2 planning intact. Flagged as emotionally load-bearing for PM revisit.
- **67-monster lore constraint** preserved as a hard glossary/scope fact; MVP ships only a subset (3–5 per design doc) of the 67-monster universe.
- **Credit economy** taken verbatim from CLAUDE.md/design doc as `[ASSUMPTION: subject to balancing]` since the design doc itself flags costs as "subject to balancing".
- **Backend** (Firebase) treated as optional for MVP per CLAUDE.md; MVP assumes local-only progression with server-side purchase verification flagged as a strong recommendation, not a hard MVP gate. Logged as Open Question.
