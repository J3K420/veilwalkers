Three slot states reveal scope without spoiling dread:
Discovered = full chunky art + name + tier badge.
Silhouette (???) = a black shape + tier badge for monsters in tiers you've begun to encounter — you know it's out there.
Hidden (?) = flat empty slot, no shape, no badge. The scariest top-tier (Epic / Nightmare) monsters stay totally mysterious until you get close.

GLOOMSTAG

A stag made of leftover dusk, the Gloomstag wanders parking lots at the exact moment the streetlights flicker on. Folks say its antlers aren't antlers at all, but the bare branches of a tree that hasn't existed in two hundred years. It won't hurt you — it just wants you to remember something you'd rather forget.

The Codex Reveal

On first capture, the slot flips from silhouette to full art with a CAUGHT! stamp slamming down in the monster's tier color, and the counter ticks 11 / 67 → 12 / 67. When a new TIER unlocks, extra silhouettes fade into the grid one by one — "the Veil shows you more…" — teasing the dread that's now within reach.
